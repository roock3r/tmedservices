About Us

Transport Medical Service is a trusted home healthcare service provider with a difference.

Transport Medical Service is an extension of the care provided to patients in hospitals; the specialists and doctors consider the patient’s house as an additional ward at home, wherein the responsibility of the patient’s care continues to be entrusted with them. The core purpose is to deliver 'hospital like care in the comfort of one’s home. Transport Medical Service is backed by clinical excellence and proven track record of the Private medical centers in Belize, pioneers in healthcare delivery, with a vast experience of over a decade. The Transport Medical Service team is trained, guided and supported by highly reputed clinicians and allied healthcare providers.
